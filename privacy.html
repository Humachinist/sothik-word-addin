Plain Language Summary

  • Sothik does not send your document content to any server

Section 1

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the "Sothik" (সঠিক) Bangla Spell and Grammar Checker, a Microsoft Word add-in developed and maintained by Mohammad Shahidul Islam ("we," "our," or "the Developer").

This policy explains what information we collect (or do not collect), how it is used, and what rights you have as a user of this add-in.

By installing or using the Sothik add-in, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy.

Section 2

Information We Do Not Collect

The Sothik add-in does not collect, store, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • The text or contents of your Word documents
  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifiers
  • Your Microsoft account credentials or profile information
  • Your device information, operating system, or hardware details
  • Your IP address or geographic location
  • Usage analytics, behavioral data, or interaction history
  • Any cookies, tracking identifiers, or session tokens
Section 3

How the Add-in Works

Sothik operates entirely within your Microsoft Word application. Its processing model is designed to be private by default:

The text in your document is processed within your Word session on your own device. Spell-checking and grammar analysis results are displayed directly in the task pane. No document content is transmitted to any external server at any point.

The add-in only modifies your document when you explicitly choose to apply a correction. No automated or background changes are made to your document without your action.

Section 4

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, trade, or otherwise disclose any personal information or document content to any third party, including but not limited to advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or government agencies (except where required by applicable law).

The add-in is distributed through Microsoft AppSource. Microsoft's own Privacy Policy governs how Microsoft handles data related to its platform and services, and is separate from this policy.

Section 5

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Sothik add-in does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or any similar tracking technologies. No persistent identifiers are stored on your device by this add-in.

Section 6

Data Security

Because Sothik does not collect or transmit any personal data or document content, there is no user data stored on our servers to secure or protect. The add-in files are served over HTTPS to ensure secure delivery to your browser or Word application.

Section 7

Children's Privacy

The Sothik add-in does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, or under the age defined as a minor in the user's jurisdiction. Since no personal data is collected from any user, this add-in is safe to use for all age groups.

Section 8

Your Rights

Since Sothik does not collect any personal data, there is no stored data to access, correct, or delete. However, at any time you may:

  • Uninstall the add-in from Word via Insert → My Add-ins → Sothik → Remove
  • Revoke the add-in's document access permissions through Word's settings
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
