How does it work?

Get started in three simple steps

Install

Download "Sothik" from Microsoft AppSource and install it in Word in one click.

Open the Panel

Click the "Sothik" tab in the Word ribbon, then press "Check Spelling" to open the task pane.

Review & Fix

Browse the detected errors, pick a suggestion for each one, and apply the correction.
