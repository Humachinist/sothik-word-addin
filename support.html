Step-by-Step Installation Guide

  1. Open Microsoft Word (Microsoft 365 or Word 2016 and above)
  2. Click the Insert tab in the top ribbon
  3. Click Get Add-ins or Office Add-ins
  4. In the search box, type "Sothik" and press Enter
  5. Find the Sothik add-in in the results and click Add
  6. A new "Sothik" tab will appear in your Word ribbon — installation complete
